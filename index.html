Who Are We?

We work closely with local & international consultant brokerage providers that have established relationships with clients within the financial services, commercial and industrial industries. With a combined 10+ years experience working internally as recruiters, talent managers and support units, we are able to navigate the complex and often murky waters and get transparent and clear feedback and insights freelancers have often missed.

What do we actually do?

With more waves of regulations posed to wash over Europe, one in particular involves transparency around wages. Banks are demanding companies disclose the pay rate and margins of conventional brokers and service providers. We are here to be ahead of the curve. We believe all brokerage services should have industry standards and we believe as brokers, our role is merely as facilitators. In order to put our client first, we should in fact not take advantage of the trust and loyalty they have granted.
